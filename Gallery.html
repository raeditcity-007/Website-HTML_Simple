During autumn like this, we can spend time like visiting beautiful places in Korea. As the southern area is cool and calm, it is perfect as a place to enjoy autumn. Even though during the covid-19 pandemic, we shouldn't be sad or miss this fall even at home. We can still enjoy it even if we just sit at home right now. Like I did, watching some of the best rated Korean dramas this year. Here are some autumn-themed dramas that are ready to fill your time.

Yoon Se Ri (Son Ye Jin) is the CEO of a cosmetics company, Seri's Choice. He is also one of the heirs to a conglomerate in South Korea. One day, while he was paragliding, suddenly a strong wind made Yoon Se Ri make an emergency landing in North Korea. There, he meets Ri Jung Hyuk (Hyun Bin), who is a North Korean military officer.

He tried to protect her and hide it. Shortly after, Ri Jung Hyuk fell in love with Yoon Se Ri. Seo Dan (Seo Ji Hye) is Ri Jung Hyuk's fiancé who recently returned from abroad. How shocked he was, when he found out that his fiancé had hidden another woman in his house. He tried to separate the two of them. Koo Seung Joon (Kim Jung Hyun) is a con artist who is hiding in North Korea, while he is there he is surprised to see Yoon Se Ri. He tries to take advantage of the situation to trick Yoon Se Ri's family.
CRASH LANDING ON YOU Meow: The Secret Boy Meow: The Secret Boy or another title "Welcome" tells of Sol-A (Shin Ye-Eun) is a woman in her mid-20s who works for a graphic design company. Sol-A dreams of becoming a webcomic artist and he is a very sociable person. Sol-A accidentally brings home a cat, Hong-Zo (played by Kim Myung-Soo). Sol-A doesn't realize that Hong-Zo is no ordinary cat. He has the ability to turn into a human. Living with a cat named Hong-Zo, Sol-A is involved in an unexpected case.
Tomorrow With You Tomorrow With You - Yoo So Joon is the successful CEO of a real estate company who has the ability to time travel when he rides the subway. Back and forth in time, he saw a future himself living a life full of torment and unhappiness. To try to compensate for his shattered future, he decides to marry Song Ma Rin, a cheerful photographer, even though he doesn't love her. Can Ma Rin help So Joon change his unhappy future?
The love search application named Joalarm has arrived in the world for the first time. The app will send a notification to the user via an alarm, when their crush is within ten meters. This app does not provide specific details or personal data of the people the user likes, but it can reveal the exact number of people the user likes.
Kim Jo Jo (Kim So Hyun) is a high school student, she is beautiful and also smart. He was someone who looked happy to his classmates. One thing they don't know is that he has a dark and tragic past that he doesn't want to reveal to anyone, his parents died when he was a child. Since then he has been living with his aunt, but he is not happy. He is unlike the others in his school, he does not download the Joalarm application.
Hwan Sung Oh (Song Kang) is a very popular handsome student, he is always surrounded by friends and is admired by many beautiful women. He also attended the same school as Kim Jo Jo. Since he was born, he has had a luxurious life that many people envy, his parents are millionaires, but because they are busy working, they never show affection or love for him. However, he continues his life thanks to the friendship he has with his best friend Lee Hee Young (Jung Ga Ram). One day Hwan Sun Oh finds out that Lee Hee Young has fallen in love with Kim Jo Jo. Due to an accident, Hwan Sun Oh finally kisses Kim Jo Jo. What will happen to this love triangle? Can the Joalarm app provide a solution to all their problems? They will soon find that finding love is not as difficult as they thought. LOVE ALARM
How Are You Bread tells a love story between a patissier genius and a variety show writer. Lee Se Young will be playing the role of Noh Mi Rae, who is the writer of the show “Top 3 Bakery Kings” and for the show, she will have to play the patissier genius Han Do Woo. But it won't be easy when she tries any method to convince him to join the show. How Are You Bread
