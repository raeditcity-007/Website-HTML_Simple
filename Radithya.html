When you are adding skills to your resume or reviewing required skills for a job you're interested in, there are two types of skills that are relevant.
Soft skills are skills that apply to every job. These are your people skills - interpersonal skills, communication skills, and other qualities that will allow you to succeed in the workplace.
Hard skills are the qualifications needed to do the job. For example, computer skills, administrative skills, or customer service skills. Find out more about the difference between hard skills and soft skills. SKILLS
