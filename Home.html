Located right in the heart of Seoul, Changdeokgung Palace’s Secret Garden offers visitors entrance to a whole new world. As hidden as it is enigmatic, the Secret Garden was a place for olden royalty to gain some respite from their duties and, by extension, the outside world. True to its name, the Secret Garden is shrouded in overhanging foliage, and seems to be encased in its own little bubble of tranquility.
